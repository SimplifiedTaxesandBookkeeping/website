About

Hi, I’m Lisa Stubbs, the founder of Simplified Taxes and Bookkeeping. I specialize in helping service-based business owners take the stress out of their finances with accurate bookkeeping and dependable tax support.

I started Simplified because I saw how overwhelming it can be to juggle client work while trying to stay on top of the books and tax deadlines. I believe finances don’t have to feel complicated or intimidating. My mission is to make the process simple, clear, and stress-free—so you can focus on running and growing your business.

My clients know me for being detail-oriented, approachable, and committed to their success. At Simplified Taxes and Bookkeeping, you’ll always have a trusted partner who’s dedicated to keeping your finances organized and your taxes handled with care.


Misson

At Simplified Taxes and Bookkeeping, our mission is simple, to make managing your finances stress-free, accurate, and reliable. We provide personalized tax preparation and bookkeeping services for individuals and small businesses.

With years of hands-on experience in bookkeeping, tax management, and small business growth, we understand the challenges business owners face. That’s why we focus on accuracy, transparency, and long-term client relationships. Helping you stay compliant, organized, and financially confident.
